Featured Temple

Payson, Utah


Built in the style of Neo-classical Architecture, and completed in 2015, the Payson Utah Temple is one of the most beautiful and unique in the world. Dedicated by President Henry B. Eyring, it is the 15th operating temple in the State of Utah.

Temple Features

  • 3 Endowment Rooms
  • 7 Sealing Rooms
  • 3,000 volunteer staff
  • 96,630 square feet

Temple Facts

  • President: Lawrence R. Duffin
  • Serves 100,000 members
  • 29 assigned Stakes
  • Contains 19 original pieces of art
