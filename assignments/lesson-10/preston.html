2019 Temple and Family History Leadership Instruction

by LDS Church News

Three Apostles will address ward and stake leaders and members with temple and family history callings and responsibilities in a live-streamed event on lds.org/familyhistory on February 28. Elder David A. Bednar, Elder Gary E. Stevenson, and Elder Dale G. Renlund of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles will provide messages of instruction and inspiration on understanding the responsibilities of your calling, how family history can be used to strengthen families, and how to organize family history service in your ward. All stake presidencies, bishoprics, stake high councilors with responsibilities for temple and family history work, Relief Society presidencies, elders quorum presidencies, ward temple and family history leaders, and temple and family history consultants are invited to view the live video stream on Thursday, February 28, 2019, from 7:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. mountain standard time, available in English, Spanish, Portuguese, and French. For those unable to view the live stream, the instruction session will also be archived for on-demand viewing on lds.org/familyhistory.
